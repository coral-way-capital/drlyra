How to find your stats

Three taps in Spotify

01
Open Spotify & go to Your Profile

Tap the Home tab, then tap your profile picture in the top-right corner to open your profile page.

02
Find Listening Stats or Wrapped

Scroll down to Listening Stats and tap See all. Spotify Wrapped works too — any stats page is perfect.

03
Screenshot & upload here

Take screenshots of whatever you see — top artists, top songs, genres. Upload them here and Dr. Lyra does the rest.

Up to 10 screenshots. More data = deeper, more personal profile.
